AI-Powered Dental X-Ray Diagnosis

Dr. Manisha Shah – Research Fellowship

Background

Dental radiographs are essential for diagnosing caries, bone loss, and more. Manual reading is inconsistent. AI can offer consistency, speed, and diagnostic accuracy.

Objective

  • Detect dental pathologies in 2D X-rays
  • Assess severity of conditions
  • Recommend treatment plans

Methods

Using 5,000–10,000 labeled radiographs:

  • YOLOv5 for object detection
  • ResNet/EfficientNet for classification
  • Data augmentation + Focal Loss

Validation & Usability

Testing AI with real dentists:

  • Diagnostic speed improvements
  • Decision confidence tracking
  • Survey feedback and accuracy scores

Expected Outcomes

Clinical Impact

Optimizes workflows, reduces errors, and brings real-time insights to dental care—especially in underserved areas.
